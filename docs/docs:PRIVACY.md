\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Privacy Policy\
\
**Last Updated**: November 7, 2025  \
**Effective Date**: November 7, 2025\
\
CanadaScanada ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in compliance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).\
\
## 1. Information We Collect\
\
### Information You Provide\
**Corrections Submissions** (Optional):\
- URL of news article being corrected\
- Explanation of correction (140 characters max)\
- Supporting source URL\
- Email address (optional, for follow-up)\
\
**What We Do NOT Collect**:\
- No tracking cookies\
- No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, etc.)\
- No social media pixels\
- No advertising identifiers\
- No location data\
- No browsing history\
\
### Automatically Collected Information\
**LocalStorage Only**:\
- User preferences (theme, language)\
- Submitted corrections (stored locally, not transmitted)\
- Checked news URLs (stored locally for session)\
\
**No Server-Side Storage**: MVP version stores all data in your browser's LocalStorage. We do not have access to this data.\
\
## 2. How We Use Information\
\
### Corrections Submissions\
If you submit a correction via the form:\
- **Stored locally** in your browser (LocalStorage)\
- **You export manually** (JSON download) and email to us\
- **We review** for accuracy and update dataset if valid\
- **Email used only** to notify you of review outcome (if provided)\
\
### No Third-Party Sharing\
We do NOT:\
- Sell your information\
- Share with advertisers\
- Provide to third parties (except as required by law)\
\
## 3. Data Storage & Security\
\
### MVP (Current Version)\
- **LocalStorage only**: Data never leaves your device\
- **No backend database**: No server-side storage\
- **No user accounts**: No passwords or authentication\
\
### Future Versions (If Backend Added)\
If we implement a backend:\
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256)\
- HTTPS only (TLS 1.3)\
- Access logs maintained\
- We will update this policy\
\
### Data Retention\
- **LocalStorage**: Persists until you clear browser data\
- **Submitted corrections**: Retained indefinitely once accepted into dataset (with attribution if you consent)\
\

Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you.\

Request correction of inaccurate information.\

Request deletion of your information (except where legally required to retain).\
\
### Withdrawal of Consent\
Withdraw consent for data collection at any time (though this may limit Service functionality).\
\
### Complaint\
File a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada: [priv.gc.ca](https://www.priv.gc.ca)\
